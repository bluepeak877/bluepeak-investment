Withdrawal Rules

  • BluePeak supports two withdrawal types: Normal Withdrawal and Investment Income Withdrawal.
  • Normal Withdrawal uses funds from your Referral Wallet and Withdrawable Wallet.
  • Investment Income Withdrawal uses funds from your accumulated investment profit only.
  • Deposit Wallet funds cannot be withdrawn.
  • Investment capital remains locked until package maturity.
  • Minimum withdrawal amount is ₦1,000.
  • All withdrawals require admin approval.
  • Only one pending withdrawal request is allowed at a time.
  • Withdrawal requests are accepted every Wednesday between 5:00 PM and 6:00 PM.
  • Requests submitted outside the withdrawal window will not be processed.
  • Users can withdraw a maximum of 30% of their current accumulated investment profit. The remaining profit stays locked until package maturity.
